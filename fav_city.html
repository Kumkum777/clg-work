My Favorite City

My favorite city is Lucknow, also called the “City of Nawabs”. It is a place full of history, culture, and kindness. There are many beautiful monuments like Bara Imambara, Rumi Darwaza, and Chota Imambara that remind us of its royal past.


The city is also famous for its delicious food like kebabs and biryani, and its beautiful chikankariembroidery. People in Lucknow are very polite and friendly. From the busymarkets of Hazratganj to the peaceful evenings near the Gomti River,Lucknow always feels special and close to my heart.
